# improved-waddle
 Strategic marketing repository for growth ideas, campaign frameworks, GTM planning, audience insights, messaging architecture, and performance optimization—built to organize smarter experiments, sharper execution, and revenue-minded decision-making.

## Overview
This repository serves as a centralized space for marketing strategy, campaign planning, messaging development, and growth-focused execution. It is designed to house working documents, frameworks, templates, and planning resources that support more disciplined, data-informed, and scalable marketing operations.

## Purpose
The purpose of this repository is to:
- Organize strategic marketing thinking in one place
- Create repeatable frameworks for campaigns and go-to-market planning
- Document ideas, experiments, and optimization opportunities
- Improve visibility across priorities, deliverables, and performance goals
- Build a more structured approach to marketing execution

## Key Focus Areas
- Go-to-market strategy
- Campaign planning
- Messaging and positioning
- Audience segmentation
- Growth experimentation
- Performance tracking and optimization
- Content and communications planning

## Repository Structure
This repository may include materials such as:
- `planning/` for roadmaps, priorities, and initiative planning
- `templates/` for reusable marketing frameworks and briefs
- `campaigns/` for campaign concepts and execution plans
- `research/` for audience, market, and competitor insights
- `reporting/` for KPI tracking, performance recaps, and learnings
